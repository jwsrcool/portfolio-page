About Me

Welcome to my creative corner on the web. I'm Vera, a professional with a unique journey that's taken me from the world of Dutch language and literature to the exciting realms of creative communication.

My academic roots in Dutch language and literature might seem like an unusual starting point, but it's here that I honed my love for storytelling, language, and the power of effective communication. My career then ventured into the HR landscape, giving me valuable insights into understanding people and their needs.

However, my heart was set on the world of creativity. I found my passion in crafting visual stories through animation, motion design, illustrations, and video editing. There's something inherently magical in bringing ideas to life through visuals and motion, and I cherish every opportunity to make that magic happen.

My creativity knows no bounds. Whether it's creating music, paintings, designing furniture, writing a book, or even crafting a website like this one, the act of creation brings me immense joy. It's this all-encompassing passion for creating that drives me forward in my journey.

As I continue to explore the vast landscape of creativity, I've set my sights on a new horizon: to become a full-stack Java developer. The prospect of blending my technical skills with my creative intuition excites me. It's a journey of endless possibilities, where I aim to create unique digital experiences that merge the worlds of technology and imagination.

Thank you for joining me on this creative adventure. Let's explore, learn, and create together.
